Our Philosophy
02 — ManifestoWhere vision becomes atmosphere

The art of composed interiors, made for the way you live.

We do not design rooms. We compose atmospheres, drawing on culture, proportion, light, and texture to shape interiors that feel collected, considered, and completely personal.

For over two decades, Align Interiors has guided homeowners, developers, and commercial clients through a complete design process: from the first conversation, through space planning and material selection, to the moment a finished space is revealed. Our work spans private residences, hospitality, restaurants, offices, ballrooms, and real estate staging across the Greater Toronto Area.

What unites every project is intention. A finish chosen because it answers the light. A millwork detail drawn for the way a family lives. A material palette assembled to tell a story that is unmistakably yours.

Shabnum Sohrab, Founder

The Align Method

A guided design journey, seven movements deep.

Every project moves through the same considered sequence, scaled to its complexity. The intent is clarity: you should always know where we are, where we are going, and why each decision is being made.

i.

Discover

A private consultation to understand your goals, lifestyle, and the space itself.

ii.

Conceptualize

Mood, palette, and design language drawn from your story and the architecture.

iii.

Source

Materials, furnishings, lighting, and finishes selected against the concept.

iv.

Design

Space plans, elevations, millwork, and 3D renderings developed in full.

v.

Coordinate

Trades, contractors, and suppliers aligned to the design and the schedule.

vi.

Execute

Active site management through construction, installation, and finishing.

vii.

Reveal

Final styling, walkthrough, and the handover of a space made for living in.
